# 📊 SuperStore Sales Analysis Report (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents a comprehensive sales analysis of a retail company using the SuperStore dataset.  
The report was built in Power BI and exported as a PDF dashboard.

The main goal is to transform raw sales data into actionable business insights, helping stakeholders understand performance, identify problems, and improve decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze overall sales and profitability
- Identify top-performing products and categories
- Evaluate regional performance
- Understand customer segmentation
- Detect key business issues (low profit, high discounts, etc.)

---

## 📂 Dataset Description
The dataset represents a fictional retail company and includes transactional data such as:

- Order Date
- Sales
- Profit
- Quantity
- Discount
- Customer Segment
- Region, State, City
- Product Category & Sub-category

## 📈 Key Insights

- High sales do not always mean high profit → discounts significantly impact margins  
- Technology category generates the highest revenue, while others show lower profitability  
- Certain regions perform well in sales but underperform in profit  
- Sales show seasonal patterns with peaks in specific months  

These insights align with typical SuperStore analyses where discounts and product mix strongly influence profitability :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}.

---

## ⚠️ Business Problems Identified
- Excessive discounts reduce profitability
- Some product categories have low margins
- Regional inefficiencies affect overall performance

---

## 💡 Recommendations
- Reduce discounts on low-margin products  
- Focus on high-profit categories (e.g., Technology)  
- Optimize regional strategy and logistics  
- Use seasonality trends for better inventory planning
